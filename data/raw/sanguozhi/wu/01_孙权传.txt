孙权字仲谋，兄策之弟也。生而方颐大口，目有精光，坚为下邳令时权生。
孙权字仲谋，是哥哥策的弟弟。生来方脸大嘴，眼中有精光，坚担任下邳令时权出生。
Sun Quan, style name Zhongmou, was the younger brother of his elder brother Ce. He was born with a square jaw and large mouth, with bright eyes. Quan was born when Jian served as magistrate of Xiapi.

年十五，已为阳羡长。郡察孝廉，州举茂才，行奉义校尉。
十五岁时，已经担任阳羡长。郡里推举他为孝廉，州里举他为茂才，代理奉义校尉。
At age fifteen, he already served as magistrate of Yangxian. The commandery nominated him as filially pious and incorrupt, the province recommended him as flourishing talent, and he acted as Colonel Who Serves Righteousness.
