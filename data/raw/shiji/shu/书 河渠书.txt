夏书曰："禹抑洪水十三年，过家不入门。"
《夏书》说："禹治理洪水十三年，路过家门都不进去。"
The Book of Xia says: "Yu controlled the great floods for thirteen years, passing by his home without entering the door."

陆行乘车，水行乘船，泥行乘橇，山行乘檋。
在陆地上乘车，在水中乘船，在泥泞中乘雪橇，在山上乘滑竿。
On land he rode in carriages, on water he rode in boats, in mud he rode on sledges, in mountains he rode on mountain chairs.
