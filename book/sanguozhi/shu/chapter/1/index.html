三国志 — 第1章 先主传

文言文

先主姓刘，讳备，字玄德，涿郡涿县人，汉景帝子中山靖王胜之后也。 胜子贞，元狩六年封涿县陆城亭侯，坐酎金失侯，因家焉。

现代汉语

先主姓刘，名备，字玄德，涿郡涿县人，是汉景帝儿子中山靖王胜的后代。 胜的儿子贞，元狩六年被封为涿县陆城亭侯，因酎金事件失去侯爵，因此在那里安家。

English

The First Ruler's surname was Liu, given name Bei, style name Xuande, from Zhuo County in Zhuo Commandery, a descendant of Liu Sheng, the Jing King of Zhongshan, son of Emperor Jing of Han. Sheng's son Zhen was enfeoffed as Marquis of Lucheng Pavilion in Zhuo County in the sixth year of Yuanshou, but lost his marquisate due to the zhoujin incident and settled there.
